Jollof recipe

How to prepare

At the end, you will be able to prepare this delicious meal for yourself and anyone else

Ingredients

  • groundnut oil
  • onion
  • blended tomatoes
  • salt
  • powdered pepper
  • uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • chicken

Steps

  1. Pour oil into large saucepan. Cook onion in oil over medium-low heat until translucent.
  2. Stir in stewed tomatoes and tomato paste, and season with salt, pepper and rosemary. Cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, stir in water, and add chicken pieces. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Stir in rice, carrots, and green beans, and season with nutmeg. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover, and simmer until the chicken is fork-tender and the rice is cooked, 25 to 30 minutes.
