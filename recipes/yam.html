Yam recipe

How to prepare

This is a very simple meal to prepare and doesn't take lots of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Yam tuber
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. peel and cut yam
  2. add 4 cups of water to pot and boil
  3. add yam slices
  4. add small amounts of salt
  5. leave to cook till yam is fairly soft
  6. Yam is ready to serve with any sauce of choice
